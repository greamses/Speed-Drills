Understanding Metric Conversions

Metric units follow a decimal system based on powers of 10.

kilo (k)
hecto (h)
deka (dk)
unit (m, l, g)
deci (d)
centi (c)
milli (m)

1 km = 1000 m

To convert between units, move the decimal point:

km hm dkm m dm cm mm
2 . 5 km 2 5 0 0 . m

2.5 km = 2500.0 m (3 places right)

Converting to smaller units moves the decimal right (multiply):

kg hg dkg g dg cg mg
0 . 3 5 g 3 5 0 mg

0.35 g = 350 mg (3 places right)

Converting to larger units moves the decimal left (divide):

kL hL dkL L dL cL mL
4 5 0 0 mL 4 . 5 L

4500 mL = 4.5 L (3 places left)

Remember the pattern:

  • To smaller units: move right
  • To larger units: move left
  • Count the steps between units
  • Move decimal that many places
